MORE QUESTIONS

How long does mentorship typically last?

Mentorship duration varies based on your needs. We offer short-term assistance for specific projects and long-term relationships for ongoing development, typically ranging from a few weeks to several months.

Are sessions conducted online or in-person?

All our mentorship sessions are conducted online via video conferencing platforms, making our services accessible to clients worldwide without geographical limitations.

Do you offer group training?

Yes, we provide group workshops and training sessions for academic institutions, research teams, and organizations. Contact us for custom group packages.

What if I'm not satisfied with my mentor?

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you feel your mentor isn't the right fit, we'll match you with another expert at no additional cost.
